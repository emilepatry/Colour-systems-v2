Noise effect
Hoooold up—you're saying we can make a noise effect without shaders?

Kind of... we can use an SVG filter to create a noise-like effect.

But just slapping it over a graphic usually looks pretty dull. It's a good practice to blend noise layers with the background, or adjust the contrast() and brightness() filters.

Again, the exact blending strategy will depend on the background and the noise intensity.
